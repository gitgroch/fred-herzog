Publications

Modern Color

Edited by David Campany, Hans-Michael Koetzle and Jeff Wall

The New York Times lists this publication in the top ten photo books of 2016. A comprehensive monograph featuring 230 colour and black & white images, many never before reproduced.

Buy Now At the Equinox Gallery

Vancouver Photographs

Edited by Claudia Gochmann, Sarah Milroy, Jeff Wall and Douglas Coupland

Iconic and influential colour images of Vancouver street life from a master photographer Fred Herzog, who has spent the past fifty years documenting the city, published to coincide with a major exhibition of his work.

Buy Now at the Vancouver Art Gallery

Fred Herzog: Photographs

Edited by Grant Arnold, Michael Turner

A comprehensive collection of the published works of Fred Herzog with essays by the authors.
